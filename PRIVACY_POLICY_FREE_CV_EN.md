# Privacy Policy – Free CV

**Last updated:** July 11, 2026.

**Other language:** [Srpski](./PRIVACY_POLICY_FREE_CV.md)

## 1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how the **Free CV** application ("app", "we") collects, uses, and protects your data. By using the app and the web service at [cv.hexatech.rs](https://cv.hexatech.rs), you accept the terms described in this policy.

The app is developed by Hexatech. For privacy questions, contact us using the details in the Contact section.

---

## 2. Data we collect

### 2.1 Data you provide

- **Account:** username, email address, password (stored in encrypted/hashed form). This data is required to create an account and sign in.
- **CV content:** personal details, work experience, education, skills, and other information you enter into your CV. This data is stored on our servers so you can access it from the web app or mobile device.

### 2.2 Data collected automatically

- **Technical data:** device type, operating system, app version – to the extent needed to operate the service and fix errors.
- **Server logs:** IP address, request time, and technical error information, without profiling for marketing purposes.

### 2.3 Data we do not collect

We do not sell your data. We do not collect background location, track you for advertising, or share your data with third parties for marketing. The app does not display ads.

---

## 3. Purpose of processing

We use your data only to:

- **Provide the service:** sign-in, storage and sync of CVs, PDF export.
- **Security:** protect accounts and prevent misuse.
- **Legal obligations:** where required by law.
- **Improve the service:** fix errors and improve the app.

---

## 4. Legal basis

- **Contract performance:** processing is necessary to provide the service you accepted.
- **Legitimate interest:** for security, abuse prevention, and maintaining the service.

---

## 5. Data retention

- Data is stored on servers in Europe (Hetzner, Germany), in accordance with applicable regulations.
- We retain data while your account is active.
- Passwords are not stored in plain text; we use industry-standard hashing.
- Traffic between the app and server is encrypted via HTTPS.

---

## 6. Sharing with third parties

- **Hosting and infrastructure:** we use trusted providers for servers, databases, and backups. Processing is limited to providing the service on our behalf.
- **Legal obligations:** we may be required to disclose data to authorities when legally required.

We do not sell or share your data with third parties for marketing purposes.

---

Under applicable law (including GDPR where it applies), you have the right to:

- request access, correction, or deletion of your data;
- restrict processing or object to processing;
- lodge a complaint with a data protection authority.

To exercise these rights, contact us using the details below.

## 8. Security

We apply appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse.

## 9. Children

The service is not intended for children under 16. If we learn that we collected data from a child without appropriate consent, we will take steps to delete that data.
