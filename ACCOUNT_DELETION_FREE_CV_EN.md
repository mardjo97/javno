# Account deletion – Free CV

**Last updated:** July 11, 2026.

**Srpski:** [ACCOUNT_DELETION_FREE_CV.md](./ACCOUNT_DELETION_FREE_CV.md)

## How to request deletion

Send an email to **hexatech.rs@gmail.com** with:

- subject: `Free CV account deletion request`
- your username
- the email address used for registration

The request should be sent from the email address linked to your account.

## What is deleted

- user account
- all CVs and content stored in the account
- related personal data contained in your CVs

## Timeline

We process verified requests within **30 days**.
